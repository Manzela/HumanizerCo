# HumanizerCo — Assignment #3: three ad creative variations before scaling spend

*Daniel Manzela · Assignment #3, reasoning write-up*

# 320 × 50 — Banner.# 
We are dealing with a cold, incredibly distracted audience, so we must strip away distraction and focus entirely on our strongest value-proposition and proof point: "98% → 9% AI.". A concrete, before-and-after snapshot of the tool in action.
To ensure our ads won't be banned on Meta and TikTok, we must strip the over claiming "100% human". Visually, a 1px keyline is creating a seamless, trusted transition right to the landing page.


# 250 × 250 — Square.# 
Audiences are incredibly skeptical of tools in this space right now, so I added a transparent footer: "In-browser estimate · verify in any detector."
The premium white card aesthetic mirrors the landing page perfectly, it keeps user trust high the moment they click through.


# 1080 × 1920 — Full‑screen mobile.# 
Since this flagship format needs to land the entire story in one glance, we scaled up the proof graphic with a "Human ≤ 25%" marker. The layout strictly respects platform safe zones, pulling key elements away from top and bottom UI overlays. Visually, the white card pops against dark app chrome while mirroring the landing page perfectly.
